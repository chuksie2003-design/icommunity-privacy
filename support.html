Frequently Asked Questions

I cannot log in.

Please ensure you are using the email address registered with your community association. If you have forgotten your password, use the Forgot Password option on the login page or contact your community administrator.

My personal information is incorrect.

After logging in, open Personal Details and update your information where permitted. If you cannot edit a field, please contact your community administrator.

My dues or payment history looks incorrect.

Financial records are maintained by authorised community administrators. Please contact your administrator if you believe a payment has been omitted or recorded incorrectly.

I cannot see my attendance records.

Attendance information is updated after each official community meeting. If records appear incomplete, please contact your administrator.

Is my information secure?

Yes. OCDUK Digital uses secure authentication and encrypted communication to protect your personal information. Access to member information is controlled through role-based permissions.

Report a Problem

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your name
  • Your registered email address
  • Your device (Android or iPhone)
  • App version (if known)
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Screenshots where possible

Providing this information allows us to investigate and resolve issues more quickly.
