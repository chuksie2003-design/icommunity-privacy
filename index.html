Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 February 2026

iCommunity (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the iCommunity mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as name or email address if provided by you
  • Usage data related to how the app is accessed and used
  • Basic device and technical information

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To communicate important updates
  • To improve functionality and user experience
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only where required to operate the service or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

iCommunity is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Child Safety & CSAE Standards

iCommunity maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Any content, activity, or behaviour that sexualises, exploits, or endangers minors is strictly prohibited.

Prohibited Content and Behaviour

  • Any sexual content involving minors (CSAE / CSAM)
  • Grooming, solicitation, or harassment of minors
  • Sharing, requesting, storing, or linking to CSAM
  • Any attempt to exploit or endanger a child

Reporting

Users can report suspected CSAE or child safety concerns by emailing: support@benavilleglobal.com

Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, permanent bans, and where required, reporting to law enforcement or relevant child protection authorities.

Age Policy

The iCommunity platform is intended for community members aged 18 and above.
