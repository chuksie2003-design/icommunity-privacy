OCD Digital – Child Safety & CSAE Policy

Zero tolerance toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and CSAM.

Prohibited

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming/solicitation/harassment of minors
  • Sharing/requesting/linking to CSAM/CSAE

Reporting

Report child safety concerns to support@benavilleglobal.com.

Enforcement & Legal

We remove violating content, suspend accounts, and report confirmed cases where legally required.

Age

Intended for 18+. Under-18 users are not permitted to register.
